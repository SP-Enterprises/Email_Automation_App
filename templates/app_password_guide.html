How to Generate an App Password in Gmail

  1. Visit your Google Account Security page.
  2. Enable 2-Step Verification if not already enabled.(This step is mandatory)
  3. Scroll down to App Passwords and click it. If you don't find it there, simply click the direct link below.
  4. Log in again if asked.
  5. Select Mail and then Other (Custom name) → Name it e.g., "Email Automation App".
  6. Click Generate.
  7. Copy the 16-character password and paste it as your “Sender Email Password” in our app.
Direct link to app password setup:
https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
